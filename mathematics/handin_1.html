    5Mandatory assignment 1

    It is a prerequisite for exam participation that you hand in at least 2 out of 3 written assignments. For each assignment, all questions must be answered and there will an opportunity to receive feedback from your teaching assistant afterwards.
    Each assignment is a take-home assignment, so there will be time to read up on relevant parts of the curriculum if needed. It is strongly suggested to work on the assignment and hand it in in the study groups you have been assigned to (up to five people). The assignment must be handed in to the teaching assistant in the class you have been assigned to. Irrespective of whether the assignment is handed in individually or in a group, all names and study numbers must be on the assignment. If you hand in as a group, then all group members must be in the same exercise class.
    To maximise your learning from answering the mandatory assignment the criteria used for grading exam assignments are shown below. Make sure your answer fulfils these criteria.
    1. Method
      1. Is the correct method used?
      2. Is it clear which method/theorem is being used?
    2. Calculation
      1. Are the relevant steps shown, and are they correct?
      2. Is it easy to follow the calculations?
      3. Is correct notation used?
      4. Are any requirements checked?
    3. Solution
      1. Is the solution correct?
      2. Has the question been answered?
